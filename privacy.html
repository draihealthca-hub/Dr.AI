Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Dr.AI handles information when you use the mobile app, website, support channels, and related services.

Important: Dr.AI provides informational appearance and wellness insights. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

1. Overview

Dr.AI (“Dr.AI,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the personal information we collect, why we use it, when it may be shared, how long it may be retained, and the choices available to you.

By using Dr.AI, you acknowledge the practices described in this Policy. Where consent is required, we request it through the App or your device settings.

2. Information We Collect

Account and profile information

  • An anonymous account identifier created when you first use the App.
  • Email address, authentication details, and sign-in provider information if you secure or link your account.
  • Profile details you choose to provide, such as your name, birthday, gender, height, weight, goals, focus areas, routines, and check-in preferences.

Photos, videos, audio, and health-related content

  • Photos and videos submitted for scans, comparisons, previews, goals, or check-ins.
  • Voice recordings or transcribed voice input when you choose to use voice features.
  • Health logs, appearance concerns, notes, progress updates, and other information you submit.

Conversations and AI requests

  • Messages, prompts, responses, attachments, and feedback from AI consultations or other AI features.
  • Generated observations, plans, titles, previews, and other App outputs associated with your account.

Usage and technical information

  • Feature usage, scan and check-in history, goal activity, streaks, credit activity, referral activity, timestamps, and error information.
  • Device type, operating system, app version, language, push-notification token, and technical identifiers needed to operate and secure the App.
  • IP address and diagnostic information that may be processed automatically by hosting, security, or service providers.

Purchase information

  • Product identifier, transaction identifier, purchase status, price display, currency, and credit entitlement information.
  • We do not receive or store your full payment-card number. Apple and Google process payments through their app stores.

3. How Information Is Collected

We collect information:

  • Directly from you when you enter information, upload content, complete a scan, send a message, make a purchase, or contact support.
  • Automatically when you use Dr.AI, such as feature activity, timestamps, device details, and diagnostics.
  • From authentication, app-store, purchase-management, and other service providers when needed to confirm sign-in or purchases.
  • From another user when they enter your referral code or participate in a referral feature.

4. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Operate scans, check-ins, AI consultations, previews, goals, streaks, progress tracking, referrals, and other App features.
  • Personalize content based on the details and goals you provide.
  • Process AI requests and return generated outputs.
  • Maintain your account, sync information, verify purchases, add or deduct credits, and prevent duplicate transactions.
  • Send requested reminders, verification messages, service notices, and support communications.
  • Detect fraud, abuse, security threats, technical failures, and violations of our Terms.
  • Improve reliability, performance, safety, and the overall user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our rights.

We do not sell your personal information. Dr.AI does not use third-party advertising networks to show personalized ads.

5. AI Processing

Dr.AI uses third-party AI service providers, including OpenAI, to process prompts and content needed to generate scans, responses, plans, previews, summaries, and other AI-powered outputs.

Depending on the feature, information sent for processing may include text, images, conversation context, goal information, and related instructions. We limit the information sent to what is reasonably needed to provide the requested feature.

AI outputs may be inaccurate, incomplete, or inappropriate for your circumstances. They are provided for informational purposes and should not be treated as professional medical advice.

6. Sharing and Service Providers

We may share information with service providers that help us operate Dr.AI, including:

  • Firebase and Google Cloud: authentication, databases, file storage, cloud functions, notifications, hosting, and security.
  • OpenAI: processing content for AI-powered features.
  • RevenueCat: purchase status, product, entitlement, and credit-related purchase management.
  • Apple and Google: app distribution, sign-in, payments, purchase confirmation, and store-related services.

These providers may process information in accordance with their own terms and privacy notices. We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect users or the App, to investigate fraud or security issues, or as part of a business transaction such as a merger or acquisition.

7. Device Permissions

Dr.AI may request access to:

  • Camera: capture photos or videos for scans, previews, and check-ins.
  • Photos or media library: select and upload existing content.
  • Microphone: record voice input when you choose to use a voice feature.
  • Notifications: deliver reminders, verification notices, and important App updates.

You can manage these permissions in your device settings. Denying a permission may prevent the related feature from working.

8. Purchases, Credits, and Referrals

Dr.AI may offer one-time purchases of digital credits. Apple or Google processes the payment, while Dr.AI and RevenueCat receive purchase and entitlement information needed to deliver the credits.

Referral and promotional features may record referral codes, qualifying credit use, rewards, and related account identifiers so rewards can be issued and abuse can be prevented.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide Dr.AI, fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, resolve disputes, maintain security, prevent fraud, and meet legal or accounting requirements.

You can initiate account deletion from the App’s account settings or use our account deletion page. Account deletion is intended to remove your account and associated user data, including stored profile information, goals, scans, uploaded media, chats, and health logs.

Deletion requests are generally completed within 30 days. Some limited records may be retained longer when reasonably necessary for legal compliance, transaction records, fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, or enforcing our Terms. Residual copies may remain temporarily in encrypted backups until those backups are overwritten.

10. Your Choices and Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete your account and associated information.
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing.
  • Receive a portable copy of certain information where applicable.
  • Ask questions or challenge our privacy practices.

You may also disable notifications and revoke camera, photo, or microphone permissions through your device settings. Withdrawing consent or deleting information may limit or disable some App features.

11. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, and disclosure. These measures may include encrypted transmission, access controls, authentication controls, logging, and cloud-platform security features.

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Children’s Privacy

Dr.AI is not intended for children under 13. Users under 18 must have permission from a parent or legal guardian to use the App. If we learn that personal information was collected from a child without appropriate authorization, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

13. International Processing

Dr.AI and its service providers may process information in Canada, the United States, and other countries. Privacy laws in those locations may differ from the laws where you live. When information is processed outside your jurisdiction, it may be accessible to courts, law-enforcement authorities, or regulators under local law.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Dr.AI changes. The “Last updated” date will show when the newest version became effective. Where required, we will provide additional notice or request renewed consent.
