Delete Your Dr.AI Account

If you would like to delete your Dr.AI account and all associated data, follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

Email admin@draihealth.ca with the subject:
Delete My Account

Include

  • The email used to sign up

What Will Be Deleted

  • Account credentials
  • Profile and usage data
  • Uploaded photos, audio, and health logs

Retention

All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
